Oatmeal

Description

Easy breakfast oatmeal using peanut butter and milk

Ingredients

  • 8 tbsp. instant oatmeal
  • 3 tbsp. powdered milk
  • 1 tbsp. peanut butter
  • Water to measure

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a bowl.
  2. Add water to measure.
  3. Mix everything until uniform consistency.
